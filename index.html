Curriculum Vitae (available upon request)

Welcome!

  • I'm leading a group of scientists and engineers to develop novel Natural Language Processing (NLP) tools, the associated Machine Learning models, and the underlying software and computing infrastructure. My goal is to advance the state of the art in text understanding, retrieval and generation.
  • My research interests are in the areas of NLP, Machine Learning, and systems and architectural design for large-scale text understanding, mining and retrieval.

News

  • I'm looking for research scientists and research interns to work on Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction, Machine Learning, and Cross-lingual Information Retrieval. Please feel free to email me for more information. You can also apply here.
  • We are organizing the Workshop on Large-scale Pre-trained Language Models at NAACL-HLT 2022. Please consider to submit your papers!
  • I'm teaching Natural Language Processing at Tufts University Computer Science department.
